Secret Gossip

Required Items: New Wave Bossa Nova, Goron's Lullaby or Sonota of Awakening

Reward: Heart Piece

Mission

  • There are four seperate holes with Gossip Stones inside, you must play ONLY ONE of the three songs listed (New Wave Bossa Nova, Goron's Lullaby or Sonota of Awakening) to each of the four stones.
  • First stone is located in North Termina, to the right of the snowy patch when you leave through Clock Town.
  • The second stone is located on the left side of the Astal Observatory, located in East Termina.
  • The third stone is in South Termina, between Milk Road and the Southern Swamp.
  • The fourth stone is located a little North of the West Clock Town entrance under a rock. Blow it up with the Blast Mask or use bombs, or punch it as Goron Link.

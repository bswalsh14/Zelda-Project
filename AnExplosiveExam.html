ABC

Required Items: Clear Snowfall Temple

Reward: Ability to use Powder Kegs

Mission

  • First, you must clear Snowfall Temple to start this mission.
  • Then, go to the opening at the bottom level, left side in the main Goron Village.
  • Talk to the Big Goron inside and take on his mission.
  • Bring the Powder Keg to the area with Tingle, the map seller, and carry it up the slope to the top to destroy the rocks blocking the area and complete the mission.

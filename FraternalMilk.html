Required Items: Troupe Leader's Mask

Reward: Empty Bottle

Mission

  • On the second day, before 12PM, talk to the man laying on the bed on the second floor of the Stock Pot Inn, located in East Clock Town. You must be wearing the Troupe Leader's Mask. You can obtain this mask by completing the mission Music Moves the Heart.
  • Then, go to the ranch on Milk Road after 12PM and talk to the two brothers while wearing the mask.
  • He will give you a mystery drink which you must run back to the inn in under two minutes. Using the bunny hood makes this much easier. You can obtain this mask by completing the mission Cucco Shack's Cute Chicks.
  • Once the bottle is delivered, you will recieve an Empty Bottle.

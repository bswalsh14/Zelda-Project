Required Items: Hero's Bow and Rupees

Reward: Big Arrow Quiver and a Heart Piece

Mission

  • Go to the Shooting Gallery in East Clock Town and play the game.
  • You will obtain the Big Arrow Quiver if you score over 40 points.
  • You will obtain a Heart Piece if you get a perfect score of 50.
  • Hint: Start by aiming from the left and shoot them in order of left to right, or vice versa. This is the most efficient way.

Swamp Shooting Champ

Required Items: Hero's Bow

Reward: Quiver Upgrade and Heart Piece

Mission

  • Go to the Shooting Gallery off of Milk Road between 6AM-10PM on any day.
  • If you get a perfect score you will receive a Quiver Upgrade. If you play again and score over 2,180 points you will receive a Heart Piece.
  • Hint: Starting from the left and shooting in order to the right, or vice versa, makes this game much easier.

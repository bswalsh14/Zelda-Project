Required Items: None but Bunny Hood very helpful

Reward: Heart Piece

Mission

  • On the first or second day, go to the Postman's house between 3-6pm.
  • You can play a game where you must stop at exactly ten seconds.
  • While it is possible without, the bunny hood makes this much easier as the time does not disappear. For info on how to obtain the bunny hood, see Cucco Shack's Cute Chicks.

Follow That Scrub

Required Items: Adult Wallet

Reward: Heart Piece

Mission

  • Go to the Astral Observatory, accessable through the Bomber's Hideout, and use the telescope to look for a Deku Scrub, and watch him fly into a hole.
  • Go into the hole and decline his first offer to buy a Heart Piece for 150 Rupees and he will lower the price to 100 Rupees.

Required Items: Deku Mask and Magic

Reward: Access to the Bomber's Hideout

Mission

  • In North Clock Town, talk to boy shooting at balloon with the blue hat.
  • Put on the Deku Mask and use B to blow a bubble to pop the balloon.
  • The boy will ask to play a game, accept his offer. Your job is now to find all five boys in a game of hide and seek.
  • First is in North Clock Town (your current location), hiding in the corner.
  • Second is on top of the inn in East Clock Town. Put on the deku mask and use the flower to get up to him.
  • Third is in West Clock Town near the Master Swordsman building.
  • Fourth is in South Clock Town, hiding under a box.
  • Fifth is in the laundry pool, located below West Clock Town on the map, hiding under a box.
  • After you find all five, the code for the hideout will be written in your notebook. Talk to the kid with the orange hat in East Clock Town to gain access to the hideout.

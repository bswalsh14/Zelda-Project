Required Items: Goron Mask

Reward: 100 Rupees

Mission

  • Go to the Stock Pot Inn at 2PM on the first day while wearing the Goron Mask. Speak to the lady at the desk after the postman leaves and you will obtain a room key.
  • Inside the room you can get a treasure chest with 100 Rupees as well as listen in on a conversation on the night of the second day.

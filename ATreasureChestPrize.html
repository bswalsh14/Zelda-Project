Required Items: Goron Mask and 30 Rupees

Reward: Heart Piece

Mission

  • Go to the Treasure Chest Shop in East Clock Town and ask to play the game while wearing the Goron Mask.
  • You then have a set amount of time to get to the treasure chest in the room.
  • Hint: This game is very luck based but a semi-reliable method is starting from the far left corner and hugging the wall to the left, until you hit a dead end or the chest. If you hit a dead end, just keep following the wall, taking a left whenever you can. If you follow this method and you should get to the chest.
  • Once you reach the chest, you will get a Heart Piece.

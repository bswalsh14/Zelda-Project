A Goron's Grief

Required Items: Goron's Lullaby and Deku Sticks

Reward: Don Gero's Mask and Rock Sirloin

Mission

  • First, you must know the Goron's Lullaby, obtained through the main quest, and play it while in the Goron Shrine as Goron Link to put the baby to sleep
  • Then, light all of the torches in the shrine to make the chandelier on the ceiling start to rotate.
  • Use B with Goron Link to roll and break the chandelier. One of the parts will drop a Rock Sirloin.
  • Give the Rock Sirloin to the hungry man on the cliff in the area with the Swordsmith to recieve the Don Gero Mask.

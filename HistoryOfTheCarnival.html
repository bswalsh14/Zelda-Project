Required Items: All Night Mask

Reward: Heart Piece

Mission

  • First, make sure you have the All Night Mask. You can obtain this by completing the mission Curiosity Shop Rarity.
  • Put on the mask and talk to the old lady in the Stock Pot Inn in East Clock Town.
  • Pick the story, "The Carnival of Time".
  • After her story, answer her question with, "on the eve of the festival." to obtain the Heart Piece.

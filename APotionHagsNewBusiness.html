A Potion Hag's New Business

Required Items: Hero's Bow and Clear Woodfall Temple

Reward: Empty Bottle

Mission

  • After clearing Woodfall Temple, talk to Koume and agree to play her game.
  • Shoot the targets from under her, making sure not to hit her. Once you hit 20 targets stop shooting and wait for the game to end, so you don't risk hitting her.

Required Items: Song of Healing

Reward: Heart Piece and Kamaro's Mask

Mission

  • Go through the North exit of Clock Town to North Termina Field any time from Midnight-6.
  • You will see a man dancing on a mushroom. Go up to him and play the Song of Healing. In return, he will give you Kamaro's Mask.
  • Now go to West Clock Town, put on the mask and join the dancers. They will give you a Heart Piece.

A Royal Rush

Required Items: Clear Woodfall Temple, not required but Bunny Hood makes this easier

Reward:

Mission

  • After the main quest in the Woodfall Temple, talk to the Deku Princess and she will tell you to follow the Deku Butler to get a prize.
  • If you complete this mission you will receive the Mask of Scents.
  • Note: The Bunny Hood makes this much easier. To obtain the Bunny Hood, complete the mission Cucco Shack's Cute Chicks.

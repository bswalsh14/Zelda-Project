Cucco Shack's Cute Chicks

Required Items: Bremen Mask

Reward: Bunny Hood

Mission

  • First, you need the Bremen Mask for this mission. To obtain the Bremen Mask, see A Melancholy Melody.
  • Go to the far right house at the Romani Ranch any time between 6AM-8PM. If you are just starting the game, the ranch will only open up on the third day.
  • Talk to the man, and after the conversation put on the Bremen Mask and hold B until all 10 of the chicks follow you.
  • Once all 10 follow you, the chicks will transform into full grown chickens and you will receive the Bunny Hood.

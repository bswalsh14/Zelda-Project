Protect the Milk

Required Items: Protect Romani's Cows, Goron Mask and Powder Keg

Reward: Romani Mask

Mission

  • First off, you must have completed the mission Protect Romani's Cows.
  • Go to the Romani Ranch on the second day at 6PM and agree to guard Crimea's cart.
  • Two bandits will attack, if you defend the cart you will receive Romani's Mask.

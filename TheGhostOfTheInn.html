Required Items: Town Title Deed
Reward: Heart Piece

Mission

  • For this mission, you will need the Town Title Deed. For info on how to get this, see Business Scrub Scramble.
  • Go to the inn in East Clock Town at midnight on any day, use the flower pad to get to the roof.
  • Find the bathroom on the first floor and give the Town Title Deed to the mysterious hand sticking out of the hole.

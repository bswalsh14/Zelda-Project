Protect Romani's Cows

Required Items: Goron Mask and Powder Keg

Reward: Epona, Epona's Song and Empty Bottle

Mission

  • On the first day, use a Powder Keg to blow up the rock blocking the way to Romani Ranch. You can obtain access to this through the main quest.
  • Talk to the small girl next to the balloon and she will teach you Epona's Song and tell you to come back later that night.
  • At 2:30AM come back to the ranch to defend the house from aliens, which will be until 5AM. Play the inverted song to slow down time, making the mission much easier.
  • The easiest strategy for this is riding around the house shooting aliens when they get close. Pay attention to the dog, as it will bark at the closest alien.
  • Once you are done, you will get a bottle of milk as well as access to Epona.

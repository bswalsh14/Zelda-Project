Required Items: Rupees and the Hero's Bow

Reward: Fishing Passes and a Heart Piece

Mission

  • Go to Honey and Darling's shop in East Clock Town and win their game each day.
  • The first day is the hardest. You have to use bombchus to run up the wall and hit the targets. The second day is throwing bombs into baskets and the third day you shoot targets with your bow, which are both pretty simple to do.
  • You'll recieve fishing passes the first two days and a Piece of Heart the last day.

Required Items: 290 Rupees

Reward: Blast Mask, Bigger Bomb Bag and Biggest Bomb Bag

Mission

  • Go to North Clock Town at midnight on the first day.
  • A small clip will play and a man will steal from the old woman.
  • Stop him and the old woman will give you a Blast Mask as a reward.
  • Now go to the bomb shop in West Clock Town to buy the bomb bag for 90 Rupees.
  • Then, once you're farther into the game, go to the business scrub in the Goron Village to purchase the biggest bomb bag for 200 Rupees.

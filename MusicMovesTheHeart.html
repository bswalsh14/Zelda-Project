Required Items: Deku Mask, Goron Mask, Zora Mask and Romani's Mask

Reward: Troupe Leader's Mask

Mission

  • First, you must have the three transformation masks as well as the Romani Mask. You obtain the three masks through the main story, but you can obtain the Romani Mask by completing the quest Protect the Milk
  • Wear the Romani Mask and go to the Milk Bar, located in East Clock Town, between 10PM-5AM on the first two days.
  • Talk to the Zora by the stage and do sound checks with all four forms to obtain the Troupe Leader's Mask.
